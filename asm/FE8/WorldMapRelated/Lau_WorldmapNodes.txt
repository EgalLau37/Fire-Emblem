By default, the game is hardcoded to only check for 0x1C
worldmap nodes and 0x1F worldmap paths.  This increases these
hardcoded values.
The maximum nodes with this is "0x1C+0xC-1", but it can
theoretically be increased more as long as sufficient space
is allocated in RAM.
The maximum paths with this is around ~0x30 or 0x40.

Requires the ExpandedModularSave patch to save the additional
nodes and paths properly.
No known conflicts with other patches.

\***/
